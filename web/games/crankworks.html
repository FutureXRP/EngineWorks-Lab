CRANKWORKS · how it works

Assemble a drivetrain: power flows from the Motor through physically connected ports. Parts only fit where their ports meet. Tap a part to hold it, tap an empty cell to place it, and use the ROTATE ⟳ button to turn it.

▲Motor — 120 RPM, 12 Nm out of one face ─ └ ┬Shafts, elbows, splitters carry rotation (−5% torque each) ⚙Gearbox — reversible: 2× speed one way, 2× torque the other ◎ ✪ ▣Loads make watts — Turbines need RPM, Presses need torque

Watts = RPM × Nm ÷ 10. Meet the demand to get paid; miss it and the line stalls — no pay, but the demand waits. There's always a way forward.
